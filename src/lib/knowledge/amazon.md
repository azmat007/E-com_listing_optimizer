# Amazon AE/SA Listing Rules

## Text Rules
- Title max 80 characters (category-dependent).
- No promotional phrases ("free shipping", "best seller").
- No all-caps strings.
- No emojis or non-language ASCII characters.
- HTML/DHTML/Java/custom scripts are prohibited in text fields.
- Title/description must follow strict case rules (first letter capitalized only).

## Image Rules
- Main image pure white background: no shadows, borders, text, or watermarks.
- Product must occupy 80% to 90% of the frame.
- Minimum dimension: 500 px on longest edge (1200+ px for hover zoom).

## SEO / Search
- Include local search terms for UAE and Saudi buyers.
- Put primary keywords in the first 3 bullets when possible.
